About Us

The Youth Marine Protection Organization is a student-led volunteer initiative committed to raising awareness and taking action to protect marine environments. We organize fundraisers, campaigns, and clean-ups in support of The Ocean Cleanup.

Our Mission

We aim to inspire the next generation of ocean advocates through education, community involvement, and fundraising. We support global efforts like The Ocean Cleanup by mobilizing youth volunteers worldwide.

Projects

  • Annual beach clean-ups
  • Donation drives and eco fairs
  • Educational outreach at schools
  • Support for The Ocean Cleanup
