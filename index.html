About Us

\

\ The Youth Marine Protection Organization is a student-led volunteer initiative\ committed to raising awareness and taking action to protect marine environments.\ We focus on organizing fundraisers, awareness campaigns, and clean-up efforts\ in support of The Ocean Cleanup.\

\
\ \
\

Our Mission

\

\ Our mission is to inspire and mobilize the next generation of ocean advocates\ through education, community involvement, and active fundraising for marine\ conservation efforts. We aim to be a force for positive change by directly\ supporting global ocean cleanup initiatives.\
